About E.B. Morrison

My constant writing companion

E.B. Morrison is an MM romance author who crafts stories of love, connection, and finding home. With a background spanning from financial services to civil engineering, E.B. brings diverse life experiences to create authentic, emotionally resonant characters.

When not writing, E.B. can be found exploring new programming projects, hiking local trails with a very enthusiastic dog, or diving into the latest MM romance releases.
