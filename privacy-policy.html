Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit my website or interact with my services. I value your privacy and am committed to protecting your personal data.

Information I Collect

I may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information: When you subscribe to my newsletter, I collect your name and email address.
  • Contact form information: When you submit a message through my contact form, I collect your name, email address, and the content of your message.
  • Usage data: I may collect information about how you interact with my website using analytics tools.

How I Use Your Information

I use the information I collect for the following purposes:

  • To send you my newsletter with updates about my books, writing, and related content
  • To respond to your inquiries submitted through the contact form
  • To improve my website based on usage patterns and visitor preferences
  • To comply with legal obligations

I will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Email Marketing

I use MailerLite to manage my email marketing. By subscribing to my newsletter, you acknowledge that your information will be transferred to MailerLite for processing in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

Each email I send includes an unsubscribe link, allowing you to opt out of future communications at any time.

Contact Form

I use Formspree to process contact form submissions. When you submit a message through my contact form, your information is processed in accordance with Formspree's Privacy Policy.

Cookies & Analytics

This website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect information about how visitors use the site.

I use Google Analytics to understand website traffic and usage patterns. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual visitors. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access the personal information I hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time

Data Security

I implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and I cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how I handle your personal information, please contact me at author@ebmorrison.com.
